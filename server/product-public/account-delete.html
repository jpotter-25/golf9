Account Deletion

Delete your player account securely without emailing a password or waiting for manual processing.

Recommended

Delete from the Nine Below app

While signed in, open Profile, choose Social & Account, then choose Delete Account. The app verifies you through your password, Google, or Facebook before deleting the account.

Fastest

Before you continue

Deletion is permanent. Your profile, linked sign-in identifiers, progression, coins, cosmetics, clubs, social connections, and active sessions are removed. Completed match, moderation, security, and support records may be retained only in anonymized form where needed.

You cannot delete an account during an active online match. Finish the match first.

Web fallback

Verify that the account is yours

Choose a verification method already connected to your Nine Below account.

Player password

For accounts that have a Nine Below password.

Verified linked email

For Google or Facebook accounts with a verified email.

Need help?

If you cannot access a linked verification method, contact app-developer@potterwell.com. Never send a password.
